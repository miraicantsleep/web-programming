Foods in Samarinda

Nasi Kuning

Aromatic yellow rice served with side dishes like chicken, eggs, and sambal.

Amplang

Popular fish crackers made from mackerel or other types of fish, with a crunchy texture.

Pisang Gapit

Grilled banana served with a sweet coconut milk sauce, perfect for dessert.

Soto Banjar

A flavorful chicken soup with rice cakes, boiled eggs, and vermicelli noodles.

Ketan Serundeng

Glutinous rice topped with a mix of grated coconut, cooked in savory spices.

Kepiting Soka

Soft-shelled crab fried until crispy, a delicacy often served with a spicy sauce.
