Tourist Attractions in Samarinda

Mahakam River

The largest river in East Kalimantan, offering breathtaking views and boat tours.

Jembatan Mahkota II

An iconic bridge in Samarinda, especially beautiful at night with colorful lights.

Islamic Center Samarinda

The grand mosque with a magnificent architectural style, a must-visit for cultural enthusiasts.

Samarinda Museum

A museum showcasing the rich history and cultural heritage of Samarinda.
